The Choral Music Ministry comprises of five English weekend mass choirs, which joyfully praise and worship God through the gift of song.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Our main role is to inspire and lead the congregation in the singing of hymns and other musical responses during mass, so as to help parishioners experience a deeper and more participative Eucharistic liturgical service.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

We sing for the following Masses:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

  • Sunday 10.15am<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Sunday 12.00pm<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  • Sunday 5.30pm<\/li>\n<\/ul>\n\n\n\n

    We also sing at special feast days and public holiday masses.<\/p>\n\n\n\n
