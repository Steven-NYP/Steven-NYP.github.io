If you are curious about the Catholic faith or seeking deeper meaning in life, you might find your answer in the RCIA journey at the Church of Holy Trinity.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The RCIA is a journey of discovery who Jesus is and who we really are, and begin to understand this deep relationship with God whom we are called to.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Through Catechesis (teaching), Personal sharing (testimonies), Scripture reflection, Prayers and Songs, you will get to see and experience what the Catholic Church teaches on faith, belief and practices. Come and see a community of believers, the Church instituted by Christ himself, echoing His words down for almost 2000 years.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

In this 1 year\u2019s RCIA journey, we will walk with you to encounter Christ and experience the Catholic community with the possibility of receiving baptism (Inquirers), or be received (Christians of other denomination) into our Catholic faith.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

If you are a Catholic and wish to further deepen your Catholic faith, we warmly extend the invite, to answer God\u2019s call and discover His plan for you. Become a sponsor, or companion, and come walk with our Inquirers together!<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Come, and see!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
