The Legion of Mary was founded in 1921 by the Servant of God, Franck Duff, in Dublin, Ireland. Our objective is for the personal sanctification of its members through prayers and spiritual exercises like retreats and seminars. Legionaries live an apostolic life through prayer and work in the spirit of Mary so that others may experience the love of Christ and encounter Him personally.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What we do:
\u2022 We visit homes for the aged, hospitals and homes of parishioners asking for prayers
