Compulsory Sessions<\/strong>
1. Preparatory Session for Parents & Godparent(s) only: <\/em>11:00am, Saturday
2. Rite of Baptism: <\/em>11:00am, Saturday after prep session<\/p>\n\n\n\n

*Registration for the month closes at the last day of the previous month.
*A minimum number of infants\/children is required for the Rite to take place.
*Parents will be contacted by parish office when the rite is confirmed to take place.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The parents and Godparent(s)\/sponsors, must be instructed on the meaning of the sacrament and its obligations (Canon 851.2).
They must prepare via preparatory session(s) (whether by priest or laity), pastoral advice and common prayer.
Families might be brought together for this purpose and, where possible, each family visited.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Documents Required<\/u><\/strong>
1. Application form
2. Birth certificate of the child to be baptized*
3. Marriage certificate of parents from church* (If unable to present certificate, please make an appointment with the Priest)
4. Baptism certificates of parent(s) who are Catholic*
5. Confirmation certificate(s) of Godparents*<\/p>\n\n\n\n

(*These documents must be made available in photocopy by the closing date. Otherwise, the application will be queued for the next round of baptism)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Requirements to be Godparent(s)<\/strong>
One Godparent\/sponsor, male or female, is sufficient. There may be two (Godparents), one of each sex (Canon 873).<\/p>\n\n\n\n
