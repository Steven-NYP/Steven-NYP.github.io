We are Divine Mercy Apostolate spreading the message of God\u2019s Love and Mercy in our weekly devotion.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

What we do:
\u2022 We chant the Chaplet and Praises, and reflect a paragraph from St. Faustina\u2019s Dairy.
\u2022 We pray for the salvation of souls, especially the sick and dying.
\u2022 Our mission is to carry out the works of mercy and be compassionate to others by our Deeds, Words and Prayers.
\u2022 We conduct the 9 Day Novena to Divine Mercy from Good Friday to the Saturday before Divine Mercy Sunday, in the chapel or on zoom.
\u2022 We pray and mentor the parish level 9 Confirmands during their attachment for the Salt & Light Project.
\u2022 We organise a Day of Recollection once a year.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Social Activities:
We have Potluck, serve in canteen duty, and outings (Lenten Journey in Lent, and pilgrimage outside S\u2019pore.)<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Venue for Devotion: HT Chapel
\u2022 Divine Mercy Devotion is held every Friday at 8 pm except on public holidays.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

All are welcome!<\/p>\n\n\n\n
