Mass offering envelopes can be obtained from counter near lift lobby.
The contribution is $10\/person\/intention\/mass.
All intentions submitted are offered up for Mass, whether or not the names are displayed on notice board.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

There are three ways you could make the mass offering:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

1) Drop off<\/strong> the mass offering envelope into the metal cabinet at the notice board area.
This can be done yourself or by a friend on your behalf.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

2) Cheque<\/strong> sent by mail to \u2018Church of the Holy Trinity\u2019
Along with a piece of paper indicating your Name and contact number.
Please include the Name, the intention and the date and time of the Mass to be offered.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

3) PayNow <\/strong>via church\u2019s UEN \u2013 T08CC4049A
Under remarks, please include 1 of the following:<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    \n
  1. MC (Mass Collections): For collection during Mass.<\/li>\n\n\n\n
  2. MO (Mass Offering) followed by 1 of  3 acronyms:
    a. S (Soul)
    b. SI (Special Intention)
    c. T (Thanksgiving)
    Followed by (the person\u2019s name for which the mass is to be said) and (date of mass)<\/li>\n<\/ol>\n\n\n\n

    Example:
    <\/em>MC
    This means the transfer is for collection during mass, it could be for individual or on behalf of a group.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

    MO SI, Tan Chew Lan, 13.06.22
    This means the transfer is for Mass Offering with Special Intention for Tan Chew Lan, to be offered on 13th June 2022.<\/p>\n","protected":false},"excerpt":{"rendered":"
