Hospitality Ministry is committed to assisting everyone in our parish to have a pleasant, meaningful, and spiritually enriching experience Mass service.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

The members are expected to exhibit a genuine love in warmly welcoming parishioners and serve as role models for upholding reverence and appropriate decorum during the service.
Our key responsibilities include:
\u2013 Ushering and welcoming parishioners.
\u2013 Offertory collection.
\u2013 Distribution of necessary Church\u2019s materials.
\u2013 Exemplifying reverence and reminding parishioners to maintain a reverent attitude.
\u2013 Assisting safety, health and emergency concerns in the Church.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Timings of our ministry<\/strong>
-Regular weekend masses
-Days of obligation (Ascension, Assumption, All Saints, Christmas)
-Public Holiday masses (e.g. New Year, Chinese New Year, National Day, etc.)
-Special masses (e.g. Holy Communion, Confirmation)
-Penitential Services.<\/p>\n\n\n\n

Venue:<\/strong> Main Church or any places where we are needed<\/p>\n\n\n\n
